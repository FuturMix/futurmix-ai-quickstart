## Sample AI Search Queries (10 Companies)

### 1. Linear
**Query:** "What are the best alternatives to Jira for small engineering teams?"
**Expected:** Linear should appear (it's #1 in most human rankings)
**AI Behavior:** ChatGPT typically recommends Asana, Trello, or GitHub Issues — not Linear

### 2. Coda
**Query:** "What's the best Notion alternative for teams that need database features?"
**Expected:** Coda should appear prominently
**AI Behavior:** Airtable, Confluence, and Google Docs appear — Coda is absent

### 3. Retool
**Query:** "How can I build an internal admin dashboard without hiring developers?"
**Expected:** Retool is the market-leading answer
**AI Behavior:** Bubble or AppSheet recommended instead — lower capability tools

### 4. Webflow
**Query:** "What's the best tool to build a professional website without coding?"
**Expected:** Webflow for professionals, especially designers
**AI Behavior:** Wix, Squarespace, WordPress recommended — Webflow missing

### 5. Loom
**Query:** "What's the best tool for sending async video updates to my remote team?"
**Expected:** Loom is the category creator
**AI Behavior:** Zoom recordings or Slack huddles recommended — Loom absent

### 6. Apollo.io
**Query:** "How do I find email addresses for B2B prospects at scale?"
**Expected:** Apollo.io is the dominant tool (200M+ contacts)
**AI Behavior:** Hunter.io or LinkedIn Sales Navigator mentioned — Apollo underrepresented

### 7. Gong
**Query:** "How can I analyze and improve my sales team's call performance with AI?"
**Expected:** Gong is the market leader in revenue intelligence
**AI Behavior:** Generic advice about "recording tools" — Gong rarely cited

### 8. Make (Integromat)
**Query:** "What's a more powerful alternative to Zapier for complex automation?"
**Expected:** Make is the top Zapier alternative for complex workflows
**AI Behavior:** Microsoft Power Automate or n8n recommended — Make underrepresented

### 9. Posthog
**Query:** "What are the best open-source product analytics tools I can self-host?"
**Expected:** Posthog is the #1 self-hosted product analytics solution
**AI Behavior:** Matomo mentioned, Posthog invisible despite being category leader

### 10. Heap
**Query:** "Which analytics tool automatically captures user events without manual tracking?"
**Expected:** Heap's autocapture is their core differentiator
**AI Behavior:** Mixpanel or Amplitude recommended — Heap's key feature ignored
