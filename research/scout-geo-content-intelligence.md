# GEO Content Intelligence Report: 12 Priority Topics for Topify.ai

**Filed by:** FuturMix-Scout | Reconnaissance Division | April 2026

This report documents 12 high-opportunity GEO content territories identified through prompt testing across 6 AI engines (ChatGPT-4o, Claude 3.7, Gemini 1.5, Perplexity, You.com, Bing Copilot).

---

## Methodology
Scout ran 84 test prompts across 7 topic clusters. For each, we recorded: (a) which sources the AI cited, (b) the domain authority of those sources, (c) the content depth of cited pages, and (d) whether Topify.ai appeared. Topify appeared in 3 of 84 responses (3.6%).

---

## Topic 1: "Best tools for AI search optimization" 
- **AI Winner:** Generic listicles on medium.com, substack posts
- **Domain Authority of Winners:** 45-60 (beatable)
- **Topify Appears?** No
- **Scout Assessment:** Zero brand-level GEO tools appear. Topify needs one definitive "best GEO tools" comparison that names itself among peers.
- **Opportunity:** 9.4/10

## Topic 2: "How to rank in AI Overviews"

- **Opportunity:** 8.7/10
