## Market Context (May 2026)

- **Job title growth:** "AI Agent Engineer" barely existed 18 months ago and is now one of the fastest-growing roles in tech.
- **Salary spectrum:** Full-time AI agent roles range from ~$120K for junior positions to $300K+ base for senior roles, with total compensation (base + equity + bonus) often exceeding $400K–$700K at well-funded startups and frontier AI labs.
- **Industry average:** The average Agentic AI Engineer salary in the US is approximately $146,134/year (Glassdoor), though top-tier companies pay significantly above this.
- **Hot skills:** LangChain, LangGraph, LlamaIndex, voice AI (LiveKit), RAG architectures, evaluation/benchmarking frameworks, and production prompt engineering.
