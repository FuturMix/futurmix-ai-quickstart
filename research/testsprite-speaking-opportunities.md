## Submission Strategy

For each conference, TestSprite's optimal talk format:
- **Title hook**: Lead with the pain (manual testing is slow/expensive)
- **Proof point**: Real metrics (e.g., "Generated 10,000 test cases for 50 repos — here's what we learned")
- **Live demo**: Shows AI analyzing a real codebase in real-time
- **Takeaway**: Specific action attendees can take within 24 hours

## CFP Tips for AI Testing Companies

1. **Target testing-specific conferences** (StarEast, TestBash, EuroSTAR) — higher acceptance rate for QA topics
2. **Dev-focused conferences** (QCon, Devoxx) want practical case studies, not product pitches
3. **DevOps conferences** (KubeCon, All Day DevOps) want CI/CD integration angle
4. **Submit 3 months ahead** — most top-tier conferences have 6-12 month lead times
