## Copywriting Notes

**What makes these templates effective:**
1. **Specific pain + specific competitor** — personalization drives 3x reply rates vs generic outreach
2. **Data credibility** — concrete numbers (73%, 2.4x, $2.3M) establish authority without being salesy
3. **Low-commitment CTAs** — audit offers and "before we get on a call" framing reduce friction
4. **Reason to act now** — each email gives a specific urgency trigger without manufactured pressure
5. **Founder voice** — conversational, direct, no marketing speak
